ICS PATHWAY

The College of Computer Studies is responsible for producing academically qualified and competent IT professionals, as well as technically capable individuals, who are ready to adapt to a quickly changing and complicated technological society while adhering to norms and values. The College of Computer Studies aspires to be known as a center of ICT education, producing graduates with relevant and globally applicable knowledge and abilities.
